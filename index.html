About Me

I graduated from Stanford University with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering in 2014, and now work as a system level design engineer at Sensei Ag.

I'm passionate about empowering the next generation of diverse tech talent, as well as applying innovative technology and design principals to nonprofit organizations and social enterprises! Outside of my role at Sensei Ag, I serve as a tech R&D and design consultant for nonprofits.

In my free time, I mentor high school students/hopeful entrepreneurs through BUILD.org, volunteer with KIPP, and organize partnerships, events, and volunteering with local LGBTQ nonprofits and organizations. I can also be found exploring opportunities in social enterprise, and improving my onewheel or surfing skills.
