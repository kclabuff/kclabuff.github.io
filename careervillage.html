CareerVillage.org

Problem

Stressed and confused students need a means to get access to personalized career and college advice in order to improve their chances of future success.

CareerVillage.org is on a mission to democratize access to career and college advice. As their first product and design hire, my role is to continuously learn, and improve our crowdsourcing platform.

My role developed from a designer and developer to a team lead across the entire product pipeline. I directly managed a remote software development team of 3, while also managing contract designers.

Process

  • Research and needfinding
  • Sketching and brainstorming around many whiteboards
  • Sketching wireframes on iPad (Paper App)
  • Refine in Sketch
  • Prototyping in paper, and inVision
  • User Testing!
  • Refining prototypes & repeat testing
  • Create final mockups in Sketch
  • Handoff to Development team (create milestone and issues in git)
  • Lead Development team in daily scrum meetings
  • Ongoing testing on staging site during development
  • Updates and fixes with user testing
  • Deploy to production and continuously improve and test

Tools

  • Brainstorming and ideation facilitation
  • iPad sketching (Paper App)
  • SketchApp
  • Sketch Measure Plugin
  • Rapid prototyping in inVision
  • Product Management in Git and ZenHub
  • Teamwork

Outcome

Successful shift of product development cycle to be based on agile methodology, and organized in kanban flows through Zenhub (git) and Trello for design phases. This resulted in increased ability to scale the development and design team, as well as provide product development transparency to the rest of the team!

Created, scaled, and executed the product roadmap using ProdPad, Google Analytics, and collecting and analyzing user data insights

Led monthly team ideation sessions and product brainstorms

Led a growing team of 3 remote developers, and multiple contract designers

Project manager for successful re-branding project that was application wide

Launched new product features:

  • Question Sort and Filter - 2.6 million online learners and professionals can now sort and filter content on feeds
  • Answer Next Steps - Professionals can leave tasks or small actionable steps on answers
  • Career Goals and Tasks - Students can save advice and start to build a career plan on the site
  • User Affiliations - Ability to add a school, group, or location feed to your profile

Looking Forward

My work at CareerVillage.org focused on my ideal career path at the intersection of design, technology, and social impact.

It was an amazing opportunity cultivate and develop my creative leadership style, and provide a bridge between the core CareerVillage.org team, the technical development team, and the design team as we continue to scale and grow as an organization.
